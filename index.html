22.033 Nuclear Systems Design Project

In this project, we have created a new type of neutron detector, using new innovations in electronics and physical design. On the device, the modified BPW photodiodes produce a signal that is proportional to background neutron counts.

The physical design is meant to be easy for the farming community to use.

Note: The design is in the prototype stage and subject to a variety of changes.

Simulations

We used Geant4, a gold standard toolkit in simulating particle tracks.

Sample geometry similar to soil conditions was tested for a variety of detector depths and soil characteristics.
